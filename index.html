About Me

My name is Carlos. I have a Bachelor and Master of Science in Physics with a specialty in the field of simulations. I studied in Universidad de Buenos Aires. I finished my studies in December 2017.

In my thesis I worked on simulating a chaotic system using Fortran, making mathematical models of a specific physical system, analysing its properties and visualising different kinds of data. During this work my team and I made a publication: Role of short periodic orbits in quantum maps with continuous openings.

While working as an assistant professor (2018) at the University of Buenos Aires I gained experience using Python on several molecular dynamics models (Hartree-Fock, DFT, etc.).

I have experience in web development (HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript). I made this website myself, and a couple others. You can check my portfolio for more on my work.

Here you can see some of the technologies I use regularly (and not so much). I mostly work with Python for my projects.
