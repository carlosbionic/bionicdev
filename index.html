About Me

I have a Bachelor and Master of Science in Physics with a specialty in the field of simulations. In my thesis I worked on simulating a chaotic system using Fortran, making mathematical models of a specific physical system, analysing its properties and visualising different kinds of data. While working as an assistant professor at the University of Buenos Aires I gained experience using Python on several molecular dynamics models (Hartree-Fock, DFT, etc.).

I also have experience in web development (HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript).
