Physics is a beautiful discipline, my favourite of the exact sciences.

The Feynman Lectures on Physics

The Feynman Lectures on Physics are 3 books written after a series of lectures were given by Richard Feynman from 1961 to 1963. The first one is about Mechanics, the second about Electromagnetism and the third about Quantum Mechanics. Richard Feynman is one of the most famous Physicist and teachers because the way and methods he uses in his lectures. He is fun and very creative in his explanations. When reading the books, there aren't many equations, but the focus is in understanding the concepts more than the maths.

Summary

To summarize, we can safely say that Physics is an exact science. But we need to be sure we understand what exact really mean in this context.

  • Assumptions are made for a specific problem, but this assumptions are not always exact (perfect).
  • We make deductions from those assumptions.
  • Deductions are made in a Mathematical way, so those are exact.
