Is Physics an exact science?

Posted on March. 27, 2022

By Carlos

A long time ago, around the year 2005 I decided part of my professional future. While I was on high school (and way before that) I had a somewhat passion about Mathematics. But on the last two or three years of high school I started to learn the basics of what is called Physics. By that time, I was fascinated by the fact that Mathematics can be directly applied to everyday situations like a heavy body falling to the ground. You can compute the time it takes to reach the ground knowing the value of the gravity.

But as time went by, I realized that my teachers kept insisting that practically everything in Physics was approximated. The value of the gravity was approximated, the position of an object was approximated (basically it depends on the object used to measure its position), etc. So I began wondering, is Physics an exact science?
